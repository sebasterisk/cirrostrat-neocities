Tanner and Thames is a comic about the lives of two brothers... at least, that's how it starts.

made for fun, expressed using 400x400 pixel doodles. volume I (chapters 1–16) was drawn through the summer of 2023. chapters thereafter are part of volume II which began in the fall of 2024, still a work in progress.
